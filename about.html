FIG. – Festival for Illustration and Graphics is an annual event in Sofia, Bulgaria. From our very first event in 2021, we strive to show the full potential of the arts of illustration and graphics by always challenging and rediscovering their meaning. We look at FIG. as something more than a festival – a platform for experimentation, supporting emerging artists and annually publishing artists’ books and editions.
