FIG. – Festival for Illustration and Graphics is an annual event in Sofia, Bulgaria that originated in 2021. Since then, FIG. has become more than just a festival – a community of artists and art enthusiasts passionate about graphic arts and illustration gather around and experiment with the boundaries of censorship, artistic style and mediums. We thrive by looking into the arts of illustration and graphics as a playground where everyone is welcome to explore their meaning on their own through a curated programme of exhibitions, workshops and lectures by local and international participants.

The concept of our third festival edition will put on a test our understanding of the “typo”. The typo – not so much as an error we unconsciously do but as a chance to see things from a new perspective. The 10-day programme will unfold in 11 spaces, exploring this notion with a series of exhibitions, film screenings, workshops (for type design, risograph + screen print, zines, motion graphics design) and many more events.
